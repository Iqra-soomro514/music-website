Sidhu Moose Wala

Indian singer and writer

Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, known professionally as Sidhu Moose Wala, was an Indian singer and rapper. He worked predominantly in Punjabi-language music and cinema. Moose Wala is considered to be one of the most influential and successful Punjabi rappers of all time and to many, amongst the greatest and most controversial Punjabi artists of all time.
