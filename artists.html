Singer Bio

Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam, a Pakistani singer, is renowned for his deep and soulful voice. He rose to fame with his song "Aadat" and has become a prominent figure in Pakistani and Bollywood music. His hits include "Tajdar-e-Haram" and "Dil Diyan Gallan."

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh is an Indian playback singer known for his emotional and romantic songs. He gained immense popularity with Aashiqui 2's "Tum Hi Ho." His soothing voice has made him one of the most sought-after singers in Bollywood, with hits like "Channa Mereya" and "Kabira."

Rahat Fateh Ali Khan

Rahat Fateh Ali Khan is a Pakistani Qawwali and playback singer, famous for his spiritual Sufi songs and Bollywood tracks. He is the nephew of the legendary Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan and has delivered hits like "O Re Piya" and "Mere Rashke Qamar."
